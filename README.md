# Hyperliquid Trader Behavior vs Market Sentiment

## Objective

Analyze how Bitcoin Fear & Greed sentiment relates to trader behavior and performance on Hyperliquid.

## Methodology

* Loaded and cleaned both datasets
* Converted timestamps and aligned data at the daily level
* Merged trader data with sentiment data
* Created performance and behavioral metrics
* Compared Fear and Greed market conditions
* Segmented traders based on trading activity and performance

## Key Insights

* Trader performance varied across Fear and Greed periods.
* Trading activity and position sizes changed with market sentiment.
* Different trader segments responded differently to market conditions.

## Strategy Recommendations

1. Consider market sentiment when managing trading risk.
2. Monitor trading activity and position size alongside sentiment indicators.
3. Adapt trading approaches based on trader behavior patterns.
